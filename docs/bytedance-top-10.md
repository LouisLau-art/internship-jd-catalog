# ByteDance Top 10

This shortlist is the expanded reviewed pass over the ByteDance backend raw export pools.

- Raw sources:
  - [data/bytedance_positions_byteintern_backend.csv](../data/bytedance_positions_byteintern_backend.csv)
  - [data/bytedance_positions_dailyintern_backend.csv](../data/bytedance_positions_dailyintern_backend.csv)
- Scope:
  - `ByteIntern`
  - `DailyIntern`

Ranking logic:
- prefer `ByteIntern` over `DailyIntern` when fit is close, because you currently value the conversion-oriented track first
- prefer AI application, backend delivery, developer tooling, workflow, and quality/stability roles
- de-duplicate city clones and avoid over-ranking roles that are too infra-heavy or too research-heavy for your strongest evidence

## Recommended apply order

### 1. `AI Agent服务端研发实习生-开发者服务`

- Batch: `ByteIntern`
- Fit: `very strong`
- Why:
  - the clearest ByteDance title match for your `AI Agent + 服务端研发` story
  - strongest place to use your current open-source, MCP, skills, and shipped workflow evidence

### 2. `Dev Agent后端开发实习生-开发者服务`

- Batch: `ByteIntern`
- Fit: `very strong`
- Why:

  - especially strong if you want to emphasize CLI-agent and coding-workflow experience

### 3. `Agent后端开发实习生-Dev AI`

- Batch: `ByteIntern`
